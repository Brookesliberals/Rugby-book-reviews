About the Book

A Love Letter to Club Rugby

A memoir unlike any other in rugby literature. Stuart MacDonald — Hon Secretary of Old Northamptonians RFC — tells the extraordinary story of how the club's 3rd XV transformed from a side scrambling for players into something far greater: a band of brothers that became the beating heart of the club.

Across more than a decade, the side evolved from a scratch outfit into a group that helped define the culture of the ONs. This is the story of characters, camaraderie, humour and a deep sense of belonging that made Billing Road special.

At the heart of the book is a tribute to the people who shaped the club — including former Chairman and President Jack Doughty, whose influence continues to echo through generations of players and volunteers.

Part comedy, part tribute, entirely unforgettable — this is what club rugby is really about.

"There are rugby books about grand slams and World Cups. This is about something far more real — the lads who show up every Saturday for the love of it. Stuart MacDonald has captured something that most clubs feel but nobody ever writes down." — Reader Review, Amazon UK
